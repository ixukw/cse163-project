Progress toward United Nations Millennium Goals

The United Nations Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) is an action plan unanimously adopted by 189 countries who signed the UN Millennium Declaration at the UN Summit in September 2000, which aims to reduce the global poverty level by half (based on the 1990 level) by 2015. It is divided into 8 items.
